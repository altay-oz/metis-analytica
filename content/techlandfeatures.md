## Build Your Technology Roadmap

Stop making high-stakes technology bets on fragmented information.
TechLand consolidates patent data — IPC and CPC classifications,
applicant names, filing dates, geographies, and more — into a single,
searchable view, refreshed every week so your analysis always reflects
the latest filings. Every analysis is **deterministic**: the same
search always returns the same results, giving you a reliable,
reproducible foundation for strategic decisions — not a black-box
output that changes every time.

## How TechLand Works

**Search:** Everything starts with getting the right patents — not too
many, not too few. TechLand's boolean nested search helps you find the
right balance between **recall** (capturing everything relevant) and
**precision** (excluding what is not). This is an **iterative
process**: queries are refined step by step until your patent set
accurately reflects the technology landscape you are investigating.
Searches can be saved and shared with colleagues, making the iterative
work faster, transparent, and reproducible across your team.

**Statistical Analysis:** TechLand gives you an immediate,
multi-dimensional picture of your results. Who are the dominant
players? Where are they filing — and which markets are they targeting?
How has activity evolved over time, and which technology areas are
accelerating or saturating? Powerful filters let you compare firms,
classifications, geographies, and time periods side by side — turning
raw patent data into answers to the strategic questions that matter.
All statistical results are downloadable.

**White-Space and Cluster Analysis:** Identify where the field is
dense, where the gaps are, and which technology areas represent
genuine strategic opportunities — scoped to the market that matters to
you: USPTO, EPO, JPO, KIPO, or CNIPA. AI interpretation gives
additional context on each cluster, turning the analysis into a
strategic picture your team can act on.

**Citation Network Analysis:** See who is truly driving innovation in
a field — not just who files the most patents. Identify the real
knowledge hubs and influential players, giving your team intelligence
to act on, not just numbers to report.

**AI Summary:** Generate an instant synthesis of the key themes,
dominant players, and technology trends across your patent set — based
on your search and filters.

**Patent List:** Every search returns a browsable, reviewable list of
patents — your working dataset for everything that follows.

**Everything is downloadable:** Export your patent list as CSV,
statistical data and citation networks as Excel, and your full
analysis as an AI-generated Word report — ready for your own research
pipelines, econometric models, or strategic presentations.
